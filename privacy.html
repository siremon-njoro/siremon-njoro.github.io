MathRush Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 July 2026

MathRush is provided by Cosy Locales, operating from Kenya. This policy explains how information is handled when you use MathRush.

Audience and child safety

MathRush is an educational mathematics game intended for school-age learners. Advertising requests are configured as child-directed, limited to a general-audience (G) content rating, and non-personalized. MathRush does not use interest-based advertising or remarketing.

Information stored on the device

MathRush stores the following information locally:

  • Player name entered in the app
  • Selected grade, avatar, themes, and cosmetic choices
  • Scores, coins, achievements, learning statistics, and settings
  • Daily reward and advertising frequency counters

This information provides progress, personalization, learning summaries, and parent dashboard features. Cosy Locales does not operate an account system or upload this progress to its own servers. It can be cleared using Reset Progress, Android's Clear Storage option, or by uninstalling MathRush.

Advertising and third-party services

MathRush uses Google AdMob and the Google Mobile Ads SDK for banner, interstitial, and optional rewarded advertisements. Google may automatically process:

  • Internet Protocol address, which may indicate approximate location
  • App interactions such as launches, taps, and advertisement views
  • Diagnostic and performance information
  • App-set or similar identifiers used for ad delivery, analytics, and fraud prevention

MathRush explicitly removes access to the Android advertising ID and Android Privacy Sandbox Topics permissions. Advertising requests are child-directed, G-rated, and non-personalized. Google Mobile Ads transmissions are encrypted in transit.

Google processes advertising information under its own Privacy Policy and Advertising Policies.

Consent and privacy choices

Where required, MathRush uses Google's User Messaging Platform to provide privacy information or collect choices before requesting advertisements. A Privacy choices entry is available in Settings when additional options are required.

Internet access

Core gameplay and local progress work without internet access. Internet access is used to obtain privacy information and load advertisements. When an ad cannot load, gameplay remains available and no promised rewarded-ad coins are removed.

Data sharing and sale

Cosy Locales does not sell personal information. The advertising information described above may be processed by Google for ad delivery, analytics, and fraud prevention. MathRush does not include chat, social sharing, location tracking, or third-party account login.

Retention and deletion

Game progress stays on the device until it is reset, app storage is cleared, or MathRush is uninstalled. Advertising information is retained by Google according to Google's policies and applicable law.

Security

We minimize information use by keeping progress local, disabling advertising identifiers and Topics access, and using encrypted third-party connections. No electronic processing method is completely secure.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when MathRush features, providers, or legal requirements change. Material updates will revise the effective date above.
