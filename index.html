Built for better practice

More than a simple math quiz

MathRush is designed to feel like a game while still supporting meaningful arithmetic fluency. The app keeps learners active, gives immediate feedback, and rewards consistent improvement.

01

Classic Mode

Questions fall toward the danger line while the learner enters answers using a clear on-screen keypad. The pressure creates excitement, but the design keeps the task focused and readable.

Falling questions Five lives Streaks Shields Rush x2
02

Practice Mode

Learners can focus on one operation at a time without the pressure of losing lives. This is useful for revision, weak areas, and steady improvement.

03

Adaptive challenge

Difficulty grows gradually with grade level, accuracy, and progress so the app remains challenging without becoming discouraging.

04

Daily goals

Daily and weekly challenges encourage learners to return regularly and build a habit of short, consistent math practice.

05

Rewards and identity

Coins, badges, themes, avatars, keypad skins, titles, and answer effects make progress feel personal and earned.

06

Accessible controls

Independent settings for music, sound effects, haptics, and motion help learners adjust the experience to suit their comfort.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Who is MathRush for?

MathRush is built for learners who need regular arithmetic practice, especially learners who want a game-like way to improve speed, accuracy, and confidence.

Does the app replace classroom teaching?

No. MathRush is a practice tool. It supports classroom learning by helping learners build fluency through repeated, focused arithmetic practice.

What skills does MathRush practise?

The app focuses on addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. It is designed to strengthen mental math, quick recall, accuracy, and concentration.

How does MathRush handle learner progress?

Progress information is stored locally on the device and is used to show performance patterns, strengths, and areas that may need more practice.
